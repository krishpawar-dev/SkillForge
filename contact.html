FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is SkillForge AI free to use?

Yes! We offer a free tier with access to basic roadmaps, progress tracking, and community features. Premium plans unlock advanced AI features, mentorship, and certificates.

How does the AI Roadmap Generator work?

Simply type any skill or technology name, and our AI engine analyzes the topic to generate a structured learning path with beginner, intermediate, and advanced levels — complete with specific topics and estimated timelines.

Do I get a certificate after completion?

Yes! Upon completing a learning path and passing the assessment, you receive a verified digital certificate that you can add to your resume, LinkedIn profile, or portfolio.

Can I learn at my own pace?

Absolutely. All learning paths are self-paced with lifetime access. Learn whenever and wherever suits you — our platform adapts to your schedule.

How can I contact support?

You can reach us via the contact form above, email us at support@skillforge.ai, or join our Discord community for real-time assistance from our team and fellow learners.

Are there any prerequisites to join?

No prerequisites needed! SkillForge AI is built for complete beginners as well as experienced professionals. Our AI roadmaps adapt to your current skill level automatically.

Can I switch between learning tracks?

Yes! You can enroll in multiple tracks simultaneously and switch between them at any time. Your progress is saved independently for each track, so you never lose your place.
